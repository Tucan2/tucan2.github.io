Volunteering Opportunites







To look at volunteer opportunities for the Newark Library League or the Newark Library, look at the website at https://app.betterimpact.com/PublicEnterprise/EnterpriseSearch?EnterpriseGuid=94215a86-c03a-4c8a-b0e9-29e7222896a7&SearchType=Organization&SearchId=28645. To learn more about an activity, click on the activity. To sign up for an activity, click on the activity and fill in an application to volunteer (or log in to your account if it exists).

Here are some activites that need volunteers:

  • Book sales: setting up and running large book sales.

  • Etsy store: helping manage the online book art store.

  • Sorting and stocking books for the book store at the front of the library.

  • Helping run the programs sponsored by the Newark Library League
