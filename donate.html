Donate to the Newark Library







Donate electronically to the Newark Library League using the PayPal link paypal.me/newarklibraryleague.

Donate to the Newark Library League using cash or a check by going to the Newark Library and placing the cash or check in the red box on the bookstore shelves in the library lobby.

Purchase books from the shelfs at the entrance of the library and at book sales held by the Newark Library League to support the Newark Library League.

Purchase book art made by volunteers from the Etsy store at .https://www.etsy.com/shop/ReadAndRepurpose to support the Newark Library League.

One company that has donated a significant amount for multiple years is the Cargill Corporation.
